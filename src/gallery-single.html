CEO & President of International Airfinance Corporation speaks at the IFN Kuwait Forum on October 19

Mr. Moulay Omar Alaoui, CEO & President of International Airfinance Corporation was invited to the Islamic Finance News Forum in Kuwait. Mr. Moulay Omar Alaoui spoke and presented at the conference the topic titled “Sector Investment Opportunities for Kuwaiti Investors”. Mr. Moulay Omar Alaoui discussed the key points relating to outlook for investments and Islamic Financing in aviation.
