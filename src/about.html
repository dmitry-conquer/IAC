About Us

IAFC has been at the forefront of introducing groundbreaking Islamic financing solutions to the aviation industry. By strategically positioning itself, the company offers distinctive products and services in the Middle East, and Africa

The leadership of IAFC comprises a seasoned team with a demonstrated track record in every facet of aircraft leasing, finance, and management. This encompasses innovative lease structures, aircraft acquisition and maintenance, fleet planning, and restructuring.

IAFC's strategic focus centers on acquiring new aircraft that rank among the most sought-after globally. As an innovative and sizable aircraft lessor boasting a high-quality portfolio and global reach, IAFC is dedicated to creating value for both clients and shareholders.
