About Us

International Airfinance Corporation, IAFC, is one of the leading and fastest growing aircraft leasing companies in the Middle East.

Our expertise in global markets, coupled with robust relationships with manufacturers and leading global financial institutions, empowers us to deliver best-in-class aircraft leasing and financing services.

An innovative aircraft lessor of scale, with a quality portfolio and global reach, IAFC's mission is to create value for its clients and stakeholders.
